About us

Quartz is a global investment firm based in London and Moscow that works with Venture Capital firms, investment firms, family officesand individual investors.

We invest in disruptive technology companies that strive to become market leaders.

Our core focus are primarily fast scaling software companies with a mission to solve major pain points and solve complex challenges.

Our investment focus

ML/AI
Data - Driven SaaS
Ed Tech
FinTech &
InsureTech
Enterprise Automation

Superior performance and better accuracy are the two main competitive advantages of AI solutions. Teams that have made a fundamental breakthrough by putting AI at the heart of their decisions is what excites us and gives us confidence in solving some of the world’s toughest problems.

Software startups are helping build a data-driven society in which technology plays a major role in empowering businesses to make better decisions and improve lives.

Digitalised education becomes a new reality where people from around the world can get access to the highest quality education. Digital products and educational platforms will be the new “norm” ensuring people’s competency to be driven further.

Horizontal FinTech, RegTech & InsureTech firms who are able to show core competency in driving change, re-innovate and solve major obstacles are needed. We like to see firms that have the potential to turn into multi-layered tech giants servicing clients.

We believe that certain enterprise automation solutions will become a de facto standard in organisations, helping the workforce to upskill. The pace of automation is accelerating and more business value is generated through smart technological solutions.

With an increase of technological devices and general shift towards digitalisation, CyberSecurity is ever more important. Threats have become more sophisticated with entire infrastructures being vulnerable. As we are creating more and more value from our data we start to carry larger risks, with data-fueled applications projected to generate $13 trillion worth of new economic activity by 2030.

Managing Partner
Prior to founding Quartz, Egor worked in asset management with various HNWI and Family offices. Egor has successfully participated in dozens of late stage venture deals such as (UiPath, Palantir, Udemy, Rubrik, Klarna), and has also participated in over 90 IPOs.

At Quartz Egor Galkin is responsible for investment analysis and relations in Eastern Europe and Russia.
Managing Partner
Maxim has co-founded several startups after he entered the early-stage Venture Capital space. Prior to Quartz Capital, Maxim was working at OpenOcean where his main focus was to create and scale a venture platform, form a global DD network of data-leaders, subsequently to create a stable source of deal flow.

At Quartz Capital, Maxim is responsible for global partnerships, expanding the investor network and building new relationships to gain access to the best deals.
Non executive director
